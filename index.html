01 · The Problem

Overtourism is silently eroding Chiang Mai's heritage

Chiang Mai receives millions of visitors annually, yet no intelligent system existed to synthesise crowd data, visitor sentiment, and spatial intelligence into actionable guidance.

We built an Agentic RAG system that combines GNSS trajectory analysis, YouTube sentiment mining, and LLM reasoning to answer overtourism queries with real spatial context.
